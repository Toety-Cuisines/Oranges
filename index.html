Our Story

Bringing West Africa's
Flavours to the World

TOETY Cuisines was founded with one bold dream to introduce the vibrant, soulful cuisines of West Africa to a global audience. Starting in Canada, a true melting pot of cultures, we believe there is no better place to share the warmth and richness of Nigerian cooking.

🌍 Our Vision

To bring authentic flavours of West Africa to the world making heritage foods accessible, celebrated, and loved by everyone.

🎯 Our Mission

To develop and distribute a line of high quality, authentic, tasty and easy-to-prepare West African packaged food products.

Toety's Product

Meet OBÈ Sauce

Originating from the western part of Nigeria, OBÈ Sauce is a deeply flavourful tomato-based sauce made from tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions. Versatile enough to be a dip, marinade, or cooking sauce it elevates every meal.

  • ✓
    Use as a dip, marinade, or all-purpose sauce
  • ✓
    Completely shelf stable stored in your pantry
  • ✓
    Meets all CFIA & FDA food safety standards
  • ✓
    No major preservatives, genuinely healthy
  • ✓
    Manufactured in a world-class Alberta facility
  • ✓
    Available in 700 mL jars
